Climate Communication Channels (3C) Framework

Rationale Behind the 3C Framework

The Climate Communication Channels (3C) Framework addresses the need for systematic integration and dissemination of climate knowledge, fostering resilience and informed decision-making at community levels. It bridges traditional ecological wisdom and cutting-edge scientific research, transforming complex climate data into actionable insights for educators and communities.

Systematic Operation of the 3C Framework

The framework operates through interconnected stages, from gathering indigenous knowledge, validating it scientifically, processing this information into accessible educational resources, integrating it within educational institutions, and finally empowering community facilitators to implement practical resilience strategies.

Stage 1: Local & Indigenous Knowledge
Stage 2: Scientific Community
Stage 3: Knowledge Processing Centers
Stage 4: Educational Institutions
Stage 5: Community Facilitators

Stage 1: Local & Indigenous Knowledge

  • Stakeholders: Indigenous communities, local residents
  • Information: Ecological insights, traditional practices
